Cookie Policy

This policy explains how 805 HVAC Pro uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience while protecting your privacy.

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

Introduction

805 HVAC Pro ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website usage. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. This Cookie Policy should be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, which govern your overall use of our HVAC services and website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help our website remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to browse our site and improve your user experience.

Cookies do not contain personal information that can identify you directly, but they may store information about your browsing patterns and preferences. We use this information to provide better HVAC service information and improve our website functionality. For comprehensive information about how we protect your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How We Use Cookies

805 HVAC Pro uses cookies to enhance your experience on our website and help us understand how visitors interact with our HVAC service information. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Website Functionality

  • Maintaining your session while browsing our website
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Enabling contact form functionality and preventing spam

Analytics and Performance

  • Understanding which HVAC services are most popular
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior patterns
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Identifying which areas need better HVAC information

Marketing and Communication

  • Tracking conversion goals like contact form submissions
  • Understanding how visitors find our HVAC services
  • Providing relevant content based on your location in the 805 area

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Examples: Session management, security tokens

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our HVAC service information and website performance.

Examples: Google Analytics, traffic analysis

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your location in the 805 area) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Examples: Language preferences, region settings

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often. This data helps us optimize the website for better performance.

Examples: Page load times, error tracking

Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses third-party services that may set their own cookies. These services help us provide better HVAC service information and improve our website functionality. All third-party data collection is subject to their respective privacy policies and our overall Privacy Policy:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about website usage, which helps us improve our HVAC service offerings and content. The information collected is anonymous and includes data like page views, time spent on site, and traffic sources.

Google Tag Manager

Google Tag Manager helps us manage website tags and track important events like contact form submissions and phone calls to our HVAC service line. This helps us understand which content is most helpful to our 805 area customers.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to enhance the visual appearance of our website. Google Fonts may set cookies to optimize font loading and performance.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. However, please note that blocking certain cookies may affect website functionality and your ability to access some features of our HVAC service information.

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Advanced > Privacy and security > Site settings > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, available at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Cookie Retention Periods

Different cookies have different retention periods based on their purpose:

Session Cookies Deleted when browser closes
Analytics Cookies 2 years
Functional Cookies 1 year
Performance Cookies 30 days
