How often should I service my HVAC system in Thousand Oaks?

We recommend annual maintenance for your HVAC system to ensure optimal efficiency and longevity, especially in the 805 area's varying climate conditions. Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs, improve energy efficiency, and extend the life of your equipment. Ideally, have your AC serviced in spring and your heating system checked in fall.

What are signs I need air conditioning repair?

Common signs include weak airflow, strange noises, unusual odors, inconsistent temperatures throughout your home, or higher than normal energy bills. If you notice ice forming on your AC unit, warm air blowing from vents, or frequent cycling on and off, contact 805 HVAC Pro for prompt diagnosis and repair in Thousand Oaks.
