How often should I service my HVAC system in Thousand Oaks?

We recommend bi-annual HVAC maintenance for optimal performance in Southern California's climate - once before summer cooling season and once before winter heating season. Regular maintenance extends equipment life, improves efficiency, and prevents costly emergency repairs. Our premium maintenance programs include priority scheduling, discounted repairs, and comprehensive system inspections.

What are signs I need air conditioning repair in the 805 area?

Common signs include weak airflow, unusual noises, frequent cycling, warm air from vents, high energy bills, or moisture around the unit. In Thousand Oaks' warm climate, these issues can quickly escalate. If you notice any of these symptoms, contact 805 HVAC Pro immediately for prompt diagnosis and repair to prevent further damage and restore comfort.

Do you provide emergency HVAC service in Thousand Oaks?
