About 805 HVAC Pro

Professional HVAC excellence in Thousand Oaks and the 805 area, founded on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Founded on Excellence and Integrity

805 HVAC Pro is a trusted local HVAC company based in Thousand Oaks, CA, serving the entire 805 area with expert heating, cooling, and air quality services. Founded by Gustavo Viveros, we bring professional expertise and a commitment to excellence to ensure your home or business stays comfortable year-round.

Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient HVAC solutions with an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction. Whether it's emergency air conditioning repair on a sweltering summer day or a complete heating system installation for those cooler California evenings, we're committed to quality workmanship and energy-efficient systems that save you money.

Led by Gustavo Viveros, our team is fully licensed, insured, and dedicated to serving the Thousand Oaks community with integrity and professionalism. We understand that your comfort is paramount, and we treat every project—no matter how big or small—with the same level of care and attention to detail.
