About 805 HVAC Pro

805 HVAC Pro is a trusted local HVAC company based in Thousand Oaks, CA, serving the 805 area with expert heating, cooling, and air quality services. Founded by Gustavo Viveros, we bring years of experience to ensure your home or business stays comfortable year-round.

Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient HVAC solutions with a focus on customer satisfaction. Whether it's air conditioning repair or heating installation, we're committed to quality workmanship and energy-efficient systems.

Led by Gustavo Viveros, our team is licensed, insured, and dedicated to the Thousand Oaks community. Explore our services or contact us today.
