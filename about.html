Our Story: Excellence in Every Detail

From humble beginnings to becoming the 805 area's most trusted HVAC professionals

Founded in 2009 by Gustavo Viveros, 805 HVAC Pro began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide uncompromising HVAC excellence to the discerning residents and businesses of Thousand Oaks and the greater 805 area. What started as a one-man operation has grown into the region's premier HVAC service company, trusted by luxury homeowners and commercial property managers alike.

Gustavo's journey in HVAC began over 15 years ago, working alongside master craftsmen who instilled in him the values of precision, integrity, and relentless pursuit of perfection. These principles became the foundation upon which 805 HVAC Pro was built, and they continue to guide every installation, repair, and service call we make.

Today, we're proud to serve some of the most beautiful homes and successful businesses in Southern California. Our commitment to using only the finest equipment, employing cutting-edge techniques, and maintaining the highest standards of workmanship has earned us recognition as the go-to HVAC professionals for clients who demand nothing but the best.
